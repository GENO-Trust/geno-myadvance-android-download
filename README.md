# GENO Advance

Access salary advances, repayments, and support from your Android phone.

## Download

[Download GENO Advance for Android](https://github.com/GENO-Trust/geno-myadvance-android-download/releases/download/v1.0.21/GENO-Advance-v1.0.21.apk)

Tap the download button above on your Android phone. After the download finishes, open the file to install GENO Advance.

If your phone asks for permission to install the app, allow installation from your browser or file manager, then continue.

## What You Can Do

- Register and verify your customer account.
- Check your available salary advance limit.
- Request a salary advance.
- Track advance status, repayment schedules, and payment history.
- Make repayments and check payment status.
- Manage payment methods.
- Open support tickets.
- Receive customer notifications.

## Need Help?

Contact GENO Advance support if you have trouble installing, signing in, or completing a payment.
